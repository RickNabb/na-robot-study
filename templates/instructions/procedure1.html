Procedure

Human-Robot interactions are going to increase over the next decade, in work, home, and public contexts. We are interested in people's responses now, before such interactions become commonplace.

In this particular study we will show you a few brief scenarios that describe interactions involving a robot. Please try to imagine the situation described as if it were happening to you.

We will then ask you a few questions to capture your impressions. Please respond truthfully to each question as if the events in the scenario had just happened.
